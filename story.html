Tanta, Egypt

I was born and raised in Tanta, a city in the heart of Egypt's Delta where I spent most of my childhood. Growing up in a small apartment with my family, I learned early on the values of community impact and emotional engagement.

I wasn’t surrounded by many resources, but I was surrounded by ideas. I would spend hours reading, playing chess with my grandfather, and teaching myself about computers from borrowed books and online videos. Every small discovery felt like a victory.

Life in Tanta wasn’t always easy, balancing schoolwork, family responsibilities, and financial struggles shaped my sense of purpose. My parents divorced when I was young, and after losing my father, I found strength in helping my family and guiding my younger siblings.

Those years in Tanta weren’t just my beginning; they were my foundation. They gave me the motivation to dream beyond limits and the courage to take the first step toward a life defined by learning and impact.

El-Dabaa', Egypt

My next chapter began when I joined the Advanced Applied School for Nuclear Energy in El Dabaa — one of Egypt’s most selective STEM boarding schools. Enrolling there marked the beginning of a new phase of self-development in my educational journey.

Majoring in Electronics and Nuclear Energy, I spent my high school years working on projects that connected science with real-world impact. Through various internships and competitions, I developed initiatives ranging from building automation systems to designing AI models that promote sustainability. Each project felt like a step closer to shaping Egypt’s future.

At first, living far from home was difficult — long nights of study and the pressure of competition often tested my resilience. But over time, El Dabaa became a second home. It’s where I founded clubs, mentored peers, and led initiatives that empowered hundreds of students to explore research.

My years there redefined what education meant to me. It wasn’t just about grades or experiments; it was about building something that could outlive me. The friendships I built, the lessons I learned, and the sense of purpose I discovered became the essence of who I am.

Chicago, United States

My next chapter begins at the University of Chicago, a place I once visited and promised myself I’d return to, carrying bigger dreams and deeper ambitions.

UChicago captured my heart as more than just a university; it represents the outcome of every late night spent coding, every research paper I completed, and every challenge I turned into growth. From my small room to the classrooms and labs of one of the world’s leading universities, UChicago marks the next step in my journey, and a new beginning.
